Welcome to the Spring 2015 EC 652/CS-475 course web page. This course teaches the background and requirements of deviating from serial to parallel and distributed algorithm design and their analysis techniques. Differences between parallel computing and parallel algorithms and distributed computing and distributed algorithms will be discussed. PRAM models for parallel algorithms, different processor organization techniques, and several interesting and important parallel algorithms will be discussed. Similarly, we shall cover all standard distributed computing models and major algorithms, like leader election, consensus, and agreement. This course requires basic knowledge of sequential/serial algorithm design and analysis (pre-requisite: Design and Analysis of Algorithms or equivalent).
