Welcome to the Autumn 2014 CS 503 Web page. This course is intended to provide students advanced knowledge of computer networks and related technologies. Students are required to know the basics of computer networking, such as OSI layers, etc. In this course, we shall study traditional topics like, Internet architecture, network traffic modeling, congestion control techniques, and more recent developments, such as, overlay networks, several emerging applications, etc. We shall also study wireless sensor networks, online social networks, VoIP, peer-to-peer, and many other important technological breakthroughs in this area. We shall also learn to solve related problem sets and group projects to get a good grip on the course material.
