17.07.2017 => Welcome to the Autumn 2017 CSN 221 Web page.

 

Welcome to the Autumn 2017 CSN 221 Web page. This course is intended to provide a foundation for the students interested in Compilers, Operating Systems, and Computer Architecture and Organization. In the departmental level (for CS students), this course is a prerequisite for Computer Networks, Advanced Computer Architecture, and Mobile Computing. In this course, our goal is to help you understand the interfacing and interactions of software and hardware. With the rapid advances in semiconductor technology and miniaturization process, the CPU and memory modules of personal computers are evolving very fast and in order to cope with them, technologies of other related accesories also keep improving. We shall explore the design of different types of CPUs and memory systems (caches, virtual memory, and DRAM) ranging from the earliest ones to the latest ones. We shall also learn to solve related problem sets and group projects to get a good grip on the course material.
