Problem Sets / Class Works:

We will distribute 5-6 problem sets for you to practice your understanding of the course material. The problem sets also provide essential background material for the exams. Solutions for the problem sets are NOT required to be submitted. We shall also distribute some research papers in the Class/Tut which you need to read and understand for the group projects or the term papers.
