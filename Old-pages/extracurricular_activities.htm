Extracurricular Activities

My extra-curricular activities are mainly associated with a group called "Kolaj". This is a group of some of our school friends. "Kolaj" is just the   other way to say the word "Collage" which means an artistic composition made of various materials (as paper, cloth, or wood) glued on a surface. We are friends of different natures and colors, but as a whole we represent a real artistic composition - so we are a Collage or Kolaj, as we say it.

  This is the logo of our group. It is a face made out of the Bengali word for "Kolaj". We organize yearly cultural functions which comprises presentations of vocal songs, instrumental music, recitation and drama. We also publish a literary magazine containing Bengali poems, short and long stories, etc.

   

From left to right, the first one is the invitation card for our first anniversary program and second one is the invitation card for our second anniversary program. The third one is the front cover of our literary magazine with the same name as our group (All written in Bengali language).

First issue of our literary magazine contains the interview of the famous bengalee poet Mr. Subhash Mukhopadhyay. We organized several competitions among the local children to hone their skills of drawing, music and general knowledge. The left one below  is a snap of the drawing competition organized by us and the right one is a scene of our drama during our annual cultural function.

  

Besides several activities related with Kolaj, I personally try to spend my leisure time with books on mainly Bengali and sometimes English literature. By nature I am a voracious reader and I love to read books on various topics. I am also passionate about learning languages.
