Topics

System Development Projects

  1. Design a fully working reduced instruction set for a processor. Design and implement/simulate (using VHDL) a processor to run your self-developed instruction set.
  2. Design and implement/simulate (using VHDL) a two-way superscalar processor and include performance improvement features, such as, superscalar pipelining, branch prediction techniques, high performance cache memory, etc. For more impressive work, you can think of including Instruction Level Parallelism functionality in your processor. Design instruction sets for your processor. Compare with a market available processor of similar features and point out unique advantages of your processor.
  3. Design and simulate a cache memory with advanced features (such as pre-fetching) for providing faster access.

Survey Projects  / Technical Reports

  1. Write a survey on embedded processors for consumer electronics devices
  2. Write a survey on processors designed for smart handheld devices (e.g., smart phones, PDAs, etc)
  3. Write a survey on processor speedup techniques (pipelining, superscalar, hyper-threading, etc) adopted in different processors (with practical deployment details, challenges, etc) designed by different manufacturers for personal computers.
  4. Enhancing scalability and reliability of data centres and/or cloud based computation infrastructure. Carry out a survey of the processors being used in these large scale infrastructures and analyze their design and their properties.
  5. Write a survey on Open-Souce Computing Hardware, like Arduino, Raspberry Pi, etc.
