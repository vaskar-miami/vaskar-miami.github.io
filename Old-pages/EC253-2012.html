Welcome to the Autumn 2012 EC 253 Web page. This course is intended to familiarize students with the designing and functioning of computer system software, such as, Assemblers, Loaders, Linkers, Macro-processors, etc. We shall discuss / study in detail the machine dependant and independant features of assemblers, loaders, and macro-processors, one-pass, two-pass, and multi-pass assembler design options, loader and macro-processor design options, etc. Students will learn how to develop an assembler and to implement several important features.
